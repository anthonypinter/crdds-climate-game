Question 5: Particulate pollution

Your work on tools that detect particulate pollution includes researchers from across the world. Most of them work at institutions in the Global South with very little access to expensive subscription resources. Do you:

Publish in your preferred journal published by Elsevier, a subscription package that costs your institution $4 million per year but has a very wide readership in the Global North, since you can always provide it by email to anyone who needs the article

Pay $2,500 from your grant funds to make the article immediately available open access

% of researchers made the same decision. % did not.
